## Issue
Multiple departments not reporting operational status:

**Departments missing reports:**
1. CMO (marketing strategy, brand, growth)
2. LegalCounsel (legal guidance, compliance)
3. Accountant (financial records, reporting)
4. Bookkeeper (daily financial operations)
5. UXDesigner (user experience, design)

**Current Impact:**
- No visibility into marketing, legal, finance, design operations
- COO cannot synthesize complete operational picture
- Potential idle resources unknown

**Required Action:**
COO to establish reporting requirement:
1. Daily reports to `04 Reviews/` folder
2. Format: `{department}-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
3. Content: Status, blockers, next actions
4. Operational alignment with company goals

**Escalation:**
If departments don't report within 24 hours, escalate to CEO.
